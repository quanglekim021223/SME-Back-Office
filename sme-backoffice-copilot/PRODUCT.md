# Product

## Register

product

## Users

SME owner-operators, finance administrators, bookkeepers, external accountants,
operations managers, and system administrators who need to process financial
documents, resolve uncertain AI outputs, and understand cash movement without
losing traceability.

## Product Purpose

SME Back-Office Copilot is a human-supervised financial operations workspace.
It ingests invoices and bank statements, runs controlled multi-agent workflows,
turns model outputs into reviewable proposals, and keeps humans in control
before results affect reporting.

## Brand Personality

Calm, precise, trustworthy. The interface should feel like a premium finance
SaaS: compact, legible, evidence-first, professional, and operationally useful.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic AI dashboard decoration, unreadable raw JSON dumps, oversized
marketing typography in task screens, decorative glass effects, vague
"magic AI" claims, and UI states that hide uncertainty from the user.

## Design Principles

1. Evidence before assertion: show source text, extracted fields, confidence,
   and validation state in a way users can audit quickly.
2. Review work should be scannable: important fields come first; raw payloads
   are secondary debug material.
3. Keep the operator in flow: compact panels, predictable controls, and stable
   layouts are better than decorative novelty.
4. Separate machine proposals from human decisions: status, comments, and audit
   actions should be visually distinct.
5. Make uncertainty visible without making the product feel broken.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast for product screens. Prefer readable data typography,
keyboard-focusable controls, visible states, and reduced-motion-safe
interactions.
